The Platform

As part of the IB Curriculum, all students are meant to complete a Personal Project between Years 10 and 11. The Personal Project is meant to reflect the students’ interest. Students focus on a particular topic(s), and then investigate it in depth to create their personal project. This project, I3FY, is the outcome of the developers learning. In these 2 months, the developer has learnt the lengths of HTML, CSS and JavaScript from scratch, and then taught through the courses of this site.

  • I3FY is a student developed project.
  • The website has been designed from scratch in just 2 months.
  • The self-taught curriculum allows for flexible timing solutions.
  • I3FY aims to motivate the students through the presentation of this very web app.

We hope your journey with I3FY is smooth and happy learning!
