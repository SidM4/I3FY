I3FY - Submission Day

I3FY is near completion as developer, Siddhanth Murthy, is 2 days away from the submission date.

95 Views


This is the Sunday before school commences. I have a day to finish the whole site, but I’m not too worried. I have written out all of my content over the past 3 weeks and they are up to the standards I expected. I have all of the pages ready, except for 1. Which I’m spending the rest of the day working on. I have created templated for the Editorial Blogs and the User Blogs, but until the user sends in a blog, that won’t be used. For the programmes, the template has been created and it has to be duplicated for each session. The About Us Page is complete, just need the video, which will be recorded as a walkthrough of the site. All other site components are working as expected. I am going to be spending the day adding content into the website. It may seem like an easy task but going through each line of code to see where the content placeholders are, is a time-consuming task.
