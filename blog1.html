I3FY - The Beginning

Developer, Siddhanth Murthy, shares his experience of his first month while coding this platform.

250 Views


Let’s take a stroll down memory lane, all the way to the beginning of July ’22. We had just finished our examinations and were now diverting all our attention to the Personal Projects. It was probably the second week of July when I started this platform’s development. I had mapped out the different pages I wanted through a wireframe sketch. I somewhat knew how I wanted my page to be structured as I have been following very many UI/UX development pages on Instagram. As I needed some advanced skills in making the menu bars, sliders, and the lot, I had to rely on my technical sessions with my coach. We decided that we will begin building the site with the easier sections first and then move on to the more complex topics. Then, I began writing the actual code. Bearing in mind this was my first large scale programming project, I never thought I would end up writing more than 1500 lines of code just for the home page. Fast-forward to the 6th of August, while I’m writing this blog. This front page has turned out so much better than I originally thought. It took a whole month for this one page. Now the fear kicks in. We are less than 30 days from the submission date, and I have to finish at least 8 more pages! But, with all this stress, I still find myself inspired and motivated to keep going.
